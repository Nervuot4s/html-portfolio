The Best Movis According to Paulius

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Potter

This is my favourite movie about magic. I love interaction between characters and progression of story during each year.

Altered Carbon

Really cool sci-fi TV series. In the series, I like how technology allows people to be almost immortal and how it depicts how it can shape people's lives.

The Lord of the Rings

This is my favourite movie about fantasy adventure. I really like the soundtrack and cinematic visuals of this film and how the group of adventurers managed to complete the task despite all the difficulties.
